# AI Visibility Audit Service

**Turn AI Invisibility Into Revenue**

Find out if ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity recommend your brand to users.

---

## The Problem

40% of product searches now start with AI instead of Google. If AI models don't recommend you, you're invisible to potential customers.

## The Solution

We test **50 real-world prompts** across **4 AI models** to measure your visibility and give you a **90-day action plan** to get recommended.

---

## What's Included

- **50 Industry-Specific Prompts** (buying intent, comparisons, problem-solving)
- **4 AI Models Tested** (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity)
- **Visibility Score** (0-100% vs your top 3 competitors)
- **Source Citation Audit** (where AI gets its info about you)
- **90-Day Action Plan** (quick wins + long-term strategy)
- **Delivered in 48-72h** (PDF report + Google Sheets data)
