## Testing Protocol

### For Each Prompt:
1. **Test across 4 models**: ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity
2. **Record**:
   - Is your brand mentioned? (Yes/No)
   - Position if mentioned (1st, 2nd, 3rd+)
   - Competitors mentioned
   - Reasoning given by AI
3. **Score**:
   - Mentioned 1st = 10 points
   - Mentioned 2nd-3rd = 5 points
   - Mentioned 4th+ = 2 points
   - Not mentioned = 0 points

### Visibility Score Calculation:
- **Total possible**: 50 prompts × 4 models × 10 points = 2,000 points
- **Your score**: Sum of all mentions
- **Visibility %**: (Your score / 2,000) × 100

**Benchmarks**:
- 0-10%: Invisible (critical issue)
- 10-25%: Weak presence (needs work)
- 25-50%: Moderate visibility (competitive)
- 50-75%: Strong visibility (leading)
- 75%+: Dominant (market leader)
